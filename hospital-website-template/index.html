Services

Excellent Medical Services

Hepatitis treatment

Hepatitis is a condition that causes inflammation of the liver, often due to viral infections, alcohol use, or autoimmune disorders. At GIM Hepatology Clinic, we specialize in diagnosing and treating Hepatitis A, B, and C through advanced screening and antiviral therapies. Our preventive care includes vaccinations and lifestyle guidance to help patients maintain liver health. With personalized treatment plans, we aim to support long-term recovery and overall well-being.

Liver cancer diagnosis

Liver cancer diagnosis involves a combination of blood tests, imaging scans, and biopsies to detect abnormalities in liver function and identify cancerous cells. Doctors may use ultrasounds, CT scans, and MRIs to locate tumors and assess their severity. A liver biopsy, where a small tissue sample is examined, can confirm the presence of cancer.

Alcohol-related liver disease management

Alcohol-related liver disease (ARLD) results from excessive alcohol consumption, leading to liver damage that can progress from fatty liver to cirrhosis. At GIM Hepatology Clinic, we provide diagnostic screenings, personalized treatment plans, and lifestyle counseling to help patients reduce alcohol-related liver damage. For severe cases, we offer medical interventions and referrals for specialized care to support recovery and long-term liver health.

Gallbladder disorder treatments

Gallbladder disorder treatments vary depending on the condition, but they generally focus on relieving symptoms and restoring normal bile flow. At GIM Hepatology Clinic, we provide expert care for gallbladder-related issues, including gallstones, cholecystitis (inflammation), and bile duct disorders.
